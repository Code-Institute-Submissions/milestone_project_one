Photo Tips

When photographing small childern, get down low to their eye level, this will make for a more interesting photo.

Remember to make use of flash on a sunny day. It might seem strange to be using flash when its sunny, but if you are taking a group photo of people its uncomfortable for them to be facing into the sun.
But having there backs to the sun makes them dark with a bright background so here we use flash to brighten foreground and give a more even picture
